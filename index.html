A park for the community.

Gabriel park is an 89-acre public park acquired by the City of Portland in 1950. Its various facilities are meant to serve community residents of all ages.

  • For kids of all abilities, there is an inclusive playground completed in 2022 built with a focus of accessibility, first.
  • For the active youth and-or adult are a skate park completed in 2008, basketball courts, soccer, baseball/softball fields, lighted tennis and pickleball courts, soccer fields, and a volleyball court.
  • For a slower pace, there are accessible picnic areas, community garden, nature patch, interpretive play fields, paved and unpaved paths, and wooded trails that expand beyond the park.
  • For your furry friend is a 0.75-acre, fenced, off-leash dog park.
  • Facilities include parking lots, restrooms near key areas, and sidewalks.
